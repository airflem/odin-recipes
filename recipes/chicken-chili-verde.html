Chicken Chili Verde


Adapted from: AMY + JACKY Pressure Cook Recipes.com

Description

Chicken Chili Verde is a vibrant Mexican-inspired dish featuring tender chicken pieces simmered in a tangy green sauce made from roasted tomatillos and green chilies. This hearty stew offers a delightful balance of heat and zest, making it a comforting meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into large chunks
  • 1 pound tomatillos, husked and halved
  • 2 poblano peppers
  • 1 jalapeño pepper
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, avocado slices, lime wedges

Steps

  1. Roast the Vegetables:
    1. Preheat your oven's broiler.
    2. Place the halved tomatillos, whole poblano peppers, jalapeño, quartered onion, and unpeeled garlic cloves on a baking sheet.
    3. Broil for 5-7 minutes, turning halfway through, until the vegetables are charred.
    4. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  2. Prepare the Green Sauce:
    1. Peel the roasted garlic.
    2. Remove stems and seeds from the roasted peppers.
    3. In a blender, combine the roasted tomatillos, peppers, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, lime juice, and chicken broth. Blend until smooth.
  3. Sear the Chicken:
    1. Set the Instant Pot to 'Sauté' mode and heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
    2. Season the chicken chunks with salt and pepper.
    3. Sear the chicken in batches until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  4. Deglaze the Pot:
    1. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil to the Instant Pot.
    2. Pour in a small amount of the blended green sauce and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.
  5. Pressure Cook:
    1. Return the seared chicken to the pot.
    2. Pour the remaining green sauce over the chicken, ensuring all pieces are submerged.
    3. Secure the lid and set the Instant Pot to 'Manual' or 'Pressure Cook' on high for 10 minutes.
    4. Once the cooking time is complete, allow a natural pressure release for 10 minutes, then perform a quick release for any remaining pressure.
  6. Adjust Seasoning and Serve:
    1. Open the lid and taste the chili verde. Adjust seasoning with additional salt, pepper, or lime juice as needed.
    2. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and optional toppings like sour cream, avocado slices, and lime wedges.
