Machacado Breakfast Tacos

Description

Machacado con huevo is a traditional breakfast dish from Northern Mexico, featuring dried, shredded beef sautéed with onions and scrambled with eggs. Served in warm tortillas and topped with salsa verde and avocado slices, these tacos offer a hearty and savory start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups machaca (dried, shredded beef)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ½ medium onion, diced
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • Salsa verde, for serving
  • Sliced avocado, for serving

Steps

  1. Sauté Onions:
    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add Machaca:
    1. Stir in the machaca with the onions.
    2. Cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the machaca to heat through and slightly crisp.
  3. Scramble Eggs:
    1. In a bowl, lightly beat the eggs with a pinch of salt.
    2. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet with the machaca and onions.
    3. Stir continuously until the eggs are fully cooked and set.
  4. Assemble Tacos:
    1. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
    2. Spoon the machacado and egg mixture onto each tortilla.
  5. Serve:
    1. Top each taco with salsa verde and sliced avocado as desired.
    2. Serve immediately and enjoy your hearty Machacado Breakfast Tacos.
